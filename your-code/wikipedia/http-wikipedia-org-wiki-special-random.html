Sombrero Butte is a populated place situated in Pinal County, Arizona.[2] The location gets its name from a nearby butte of the same name,[1] so called because of its resemblance to a huge sombrero.[3] It has an estimated elevation of 4,071 feet (1,241 m) above sea level.[1]
